Form Guide

Leg Press Keep feet shoulder-width apart. Don't lock your knees at the top. Focus on pushing through your heels.
Chest Press Keep your shoulder blades pinched together against the bench. Exhale as you push the weight away.
Seated Row Pull the handle toward your belly button. Imagine trying to touch your elbows behind your back. No swinging!
Lat Pulldown Lean back slightly. Pull the bar to your upper chest, not behind your neck. Squeeze your back muscles.
Shoulder Press Keep your core tight so your back doesn't arch. Stop the dumbbells at ear-level before pushing back up.
Safety Note At 43, warm up with 5 mins of walking on the Apex track before lifting to get blood into the joints.
